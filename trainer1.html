ALONZO WILSON

FOUNDER, DIRECTOR OF TRAINING

Alonzo holds a BS Degree in Movement Science Education & Professional Instruction from Grand Valley State University. He lettered in football, basketball, and track in high school. Collegiately, he played defensive lineman for the two-time Division II National Championship-winning football team at GVSU, before moving on to the professional arena. He is currently a fitness model with the Wilhelmina Modeling Agency and was a private trainer before opening up Tone HouseR. The team camaraderie and high level of physical and mental conditioning Alonzo achieved during his formative years ultimately shaped his vision for Tone House.
